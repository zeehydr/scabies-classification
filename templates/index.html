What are the symptoms of scabies?
  1. Intense Itching: Often worse at night.
  2. Rash: Small, red bumps or blisters, some times forming lines, commonly found between fingers, wrists, elbows, armpits, waistline, and groin
  3. Sores: Resulting from continuous scratching, which may become infected.
  4. Burrows: Thin, irregular lines on the skin where the mites have burrowed.
